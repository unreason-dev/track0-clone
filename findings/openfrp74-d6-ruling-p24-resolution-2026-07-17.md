## Durable lesson

Before classifying a damaged/blank site as unrecoverable, **join it against
the errata sheet first** — a 1970s correction slip exists precisely because
the printing had known omissions, and "unreadable in our copy" and "missing
from the printing" are different failure modes with different remedies. The
errata join list built during transcription (v1/v2 findings docs) already
covered every other Correction Sheet item; this closes the last one.
